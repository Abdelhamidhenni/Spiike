About Spyke

What if all of our communication didn't have to be routed through big corporations?

Spiike uses WebRTC and peer-to-peer communcation, meaning that your conversation is in the browser(client) and doesn't have to go through a server.

Check out Documentation for more information. Spyke uses all open-source technologies.
